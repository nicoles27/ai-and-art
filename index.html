DATA USE CONSENT FORM

Permission to Track Emotions and Use Data for Research


Purpose of the Study:

This study aims to track emotions using video recordings. The video footage will not be stored. Instead, the extracted emotional data will be used to generate graphs for my university project.


What Participation Involves:

  • Your facial expressions will be recorded for emotion analysis.
  • No identifiable video footage will be stored or shared.
  • Only anonymised emotion data will be used for academic purposes.

Your Rights:

  • Participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time before data analysis.
  • Your personal identity will remain confidential.
